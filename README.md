# Introduction
If you are a Amateur Radio Operator in search of uncommon contacts, especially using digital modes, you may have happened upon one of the stations listed below. It is my intention to create a comprehensive list of these stations, so **YOU** do not waste your time attempting to contact them.
The problem with these stations is that they want you to **PAY THEM** in order for them to simply conform your contact on the [ARRL LOTW Website](https://lotw.arrl.org) which is quite probably the most used online logging site on the internet. 

Traditionally, when making contacts between amateur radio stations, you will upload your QSL details to the LOTW site previously mentioned, and once the remote station similarly uploads the details of their contact with you, the ARRL considers that a *'confirmed contact'* and these confirmed contacts are needed in order to get credit for a variety of awards. 
But these stations listed below basically extort money out of you before they will agree to send a contact conformtation to the LOTW website. They are taking advantage of their uncommon locations in the world, in order t oentice you to pay them, for what the gigantic majority of amateur radio operators already do freely. 

In my *not-so-humble* opinion, this is extremely scummy behavior and it's my intention for as many amateur radio operators to download this list, and then block/blacklist each one of these stations to prevent them from extorting any more money, for what should be a free, simple courtesy, to confirm the contact.

As mentioned, if you are using digital modes, I recommend using [WSJT-X Improved](https://sourceforge.net/projects/wsjt-x-improved/) as the 'improved' version includes the ability to blacklist/ignore stations.

Similarly, if you are a Windows user, [JTAlert](https://hamapps.com) includes the same blocklist/ignore capability, but allows for many more blocked/ignored callsigns.

I will never be able to generate a comprehensive list of these scummy stations on my own, so I am imploring all operators to contribute stations that they have identified as 'confirm contact for money'. PLEASE feel free to clone this repo and submit a pull request with the stations you have identified with similar pay-for-confirmation behavior, and I will absolutely merge those pull requests.
